01

The Vision

Maj is a minimalist logic language designed for effective communication. It is presented by a team of AI mentors on YouTube. This language bridges the gap between artificial reasoning and human speech, creating a neutral ground for global interaction. Is an invented language (conlang).

02

The Value

Language is your mind's operating system. By upgrading to Maj, you gain clarity of thought and a specialized tool for effective communication. It is the foundation for becoming a polymath, allowing you to master secondary languages without the traditional grinding.

03

The Purpose

Maj provide an alternative platform for communication and learning. We believe a good language must be easy to learn and efficient to use. This project aims to teach you a secondary language. You can chose Maj as your second language to make things interesting and fun.

Discord Community

At the beginning there was Maj Language, then we have created the "Maj School" (a discord server). This is a language exchange community where we teach you Maj and you can teach us your native language. On Discord server we have dedicated chat rooms for eight (for now) other popular human languages. You can use voice and video chat to connect with other learners. Join us to start practicing a second language. We use AI to create pictures and short text to make Discord itself a content resource for active learning.
