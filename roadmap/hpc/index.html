Computer Hardware

Before making any software, you must know what your computer is capable of doing, so that you can organize data streams to and from the computer or devices you are working with. Therefore you must learn this that may be considered by other instructors irrelevant. And do not think for a second that this is not an interesting subject.

Why we learn this?

As software engineer you need a large horizon. We cover just the fundamental aspects of hardware and networks. You need to dig deeper when you puces or create a new device or connect devices into a network. We can't teach you everything, merely help you get started.

Disclaim: In this course, we include pictures showing real products, however we do not endorse these products. Sage-Code is not a technical influencer. This is not a sponsored article and we do not represent specific products. We only explain the technical features of some representative products.
